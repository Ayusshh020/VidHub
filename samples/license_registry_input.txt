## 2. Simulated Operations & Parameters

1. Initialize empty license registry.
2. Register the 5 licenses above.
3. Attempt to register duplicate license `MIT-AJ-002` (expected: rejection).
4. Verify active status of `MIT-AJ-002`, `CC-EDU-001`, `PROP-WG-006`.
5. Verify status of unknown keys `FAKE-KEY-999` and `""` (expected: false).
6. Revoke `CC-EDU-001` (updates `isActive = false` in registry, does not delete).
7. Verify `CC-EDU-001` is no longer active but still exists in the registry map.
8. Get information for `MIT-AJ-002`.
9. Hard-remove `PROP-WG-006` from registry map.
10. Attempt to revoke non-existent key `NOT-A-KEY` (expected: false).
11. Call `displayAll()`, `displayActive()`, and `displayRevoked()`.
