# Simulated Input Data for Video Segment Manager (Deque) Test

The video segment manager tests simulate segmenting videos into buffered chunks and performing buffer operations (seeking, prepending for repair, appending live feeds, and playback consumption) using a double-ended queue (Deque).

## 1. Test Configurations

* **Video 1**: Video ID = `VID-002`, Total Duration = `2700s` (45 min)
* **Video 2**: Video ID = `VID-004`, Total Duration = `1210s` (20 min 10 sec)
* **Video 3**: Video ID = `VID-005`, Total Duration = `1215s` (20 min 15 sec)

## 2. Simulated Chunks & Parameters

| Quality | Bitrate (bps) | Calculated Size per Sec |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1080p | 4,000,000 | 488 KB/s |
| 720p | 2,500,000 | 305 KB/s |
| 480p | 1,000,000 | 122 KB/s |
| 360p | 500,000 | 61 KB/s |

## 3. Simulated Operations & Parameters

1. Initialize Video Segment Manager for `VID-002` (2700 seconds).
2. Segment `VID-002` into `10s` intervals at `720p` quality.
3. Fetch first segment (index 0) and last segment (index 269).
4. Initialize manager for `VID-005` (1215 seconds) and segment into `10s` intervals at `480p` quality (verifies partial/remainder segment handling: 122nd segment is 5 seconds long).
5. Seek to index 119 (O(1) access to minute 20) in `VID-002`'s segments.
6. Call `peekFront()` to inspect the first segment without consuming it.
7. Call `consumeFront()` twice (simulating playback of SEG-001 and SEG-002, removing them from the front of the deque).
8. Append `SEG-271` (index=271, start=2700, end=2710, quality="720p", size=3052 KB) to the back of the deque (simulating live streaming).
9. Prepend `SEG-0` (index=0, start=0, end=5, quality="480p", size=610 KB) to the front of the deque (simulating a priority repair chunk).
10. Call `displaySegments()` (displays first 5 and last 3 chunks).
11. Call `displaySegmentRange(1, 5)`.
12. Display playback progress bars for current indices 90 and 270.
13. Re-segment the video into `30s` intervals at `1080p` quality (clears the previous deque buffer).
14. Call `clear()` to empty the manager buffer.
