## 3. Simulated Operations & Parameters

1. Initialize empty Ranking System.
2. Add the 6 initial videos.
3. Call `peekTop()` (expected: `VID-004` since its score ~56,400 is the highest).
4. Call `getTopK(3)` to fetch the top 3 ranked videos.
5. Call `getTopK(100)` to verify bound handling.
6. Update `VID-001` with the "going viral" stats (expected: `VID-001` rises to the top of the heap).
7. Call `peekTop()` to confirm `VID-001` is now on top.
8. Attempt to update a nonexistent video `VID-999` (expected: false).
9. Remove `VID-006` from the ranking system.
10. Attempt to remove nonexistent video `VID-999` (expected: false).
11. Verify containment of `VID-001` (true) and `VID-999` (false).
12. Call `displayRankings(5)` to show the top 5 videos.
