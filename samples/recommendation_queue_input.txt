## 3. Simulated Operations & Parameters

1. Initialize recommendation queue.
2. Enqueue `VID-001`.
3. Enqueue `VID-002`.
4. Enqueue `VID-003`.
5. Dequeue one element (expected: `VID-001`).
6. Dequeue another element (expected: `VID-002`).
7. Enqueue `VID-001` and `VID-002` again, then display queue.
8. Clear the queue.
9. Initialize a small queue capped at capacity 3.
10. Enqueue `VID-001`, `VID-002`, `VID-003`.
11. Attempt to enqueue `VID-004` (expected to be ignored due to capacity limit).
12. Initialize a queue of capacity 5, and invoke `populate(currentGenre="Education", excludeId="VID-102", videoPool)`.
13. Display the populated queue.
14. Invoke `populate(currentGenre="Health", excludeId="VID-999", videoPool)`.
15. Display the newly populated queue.
